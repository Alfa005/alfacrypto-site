Our mission

Crypto should be for everyone

alfacrypto.ai was born from a simple frustration: most crypto content is either too technical, too speculative, or just trying to sell you something. We wanted something different.

We publish clear, jargon-free guides on the most important cryptocurrencies — explaining what they are, how they work, and what they're actually used for. No price predictions. No financial advice. Just clear, honest education.

We publish in both English and French because good information should have no language barrier. Think of us as that smart friend who happens to understand crypto — and can explain it over coffee.

Plain language, always
No jargon, no acronyms without explanation. If your grandmother can't understand it, we rewrite it.
No financial advice
We explain, we don't advise. We are educators, not financial advisors.
Bilingual EN / FR
All content available in English and French. Good education knows no language barrier.
Free, forever
Education should be free. No paywalls, no premium tiers — just open knowledge.
